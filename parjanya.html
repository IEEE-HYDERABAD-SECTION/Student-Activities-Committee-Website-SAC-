About Parjanya'21

IEEE Hyderabad Section is the best hub for inspiration, association and information in technical and executive features. Considering the Covid pandemic situations, IEEE Hyderabad Section Student Activities Committee has taken an initiation of Zonal Congresses.

The second Zonal Congress is Anantapur & Guntur Congress. It is named as Parjanya '21. This is drafted with hand-picked technical and non-technical events. This is going to be organized for the sake of improving presentation and communication skills of the participants along with technical knowledge that helps to nurture the young minds to shape their careers into a brighter one.

The main motto of this Virtual congress is to unwrap the entrepreneurial spirit and extract the creativity in students to build confidence to face and make stand for themselves in this Rapid growing competitive world.

Considering the situation of students in this pandemic, we have not only planned technical stuff but also Fun Workout break to get everyone moving with a Big Hit.

This event is used to engage & entertain the young professionals and meet the pioneers of the current engineering industry and get their guidance and suggestions of the market trends.

Here we provide a great opportunity to all the youngsters to gain knowledge and grab exciting prizes.
