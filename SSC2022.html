Buckle up for SSC'22 on 10th - 11th December,2022.

Section Student Congress 2022

Section Student Congress(SSC) is an annual flagship event of IEEE Hyderabad Section, which is a blend of technical talks, sessions on soft skills, and career opportunities, wherein student members of various Universities/ Colleges under the IEEE Hyderabad Section gather to learn, discuss and network. Last year, SSC was organized for 2 days with more than 1000 delegates from all the Student Branches and 60+ volunteers. The Student Congress aims to assemble student members of the IEEE Student Activities Committee to enable them to discuss the growth and development of IEEE student activities not only members sharing their experiences with student members. Section Student Congress is being held on 10th – 11th December 2022 with the theme of “The Era of Eduployment”.Education is a great leveler and is the best tool for achieving inclusion, diversity, economic and social mobility. One of the factors the country's economy depends on is human capital. If there's no adequate access to education and employment then we may lose half of our potential. At the end of the day the true value proposition of education is employment. This year we are going to keep the delegates engaged in a profusion of professional talks, competitions, ice breaking activities that brings out vivacity and dynamism in delegates attending the congress.
